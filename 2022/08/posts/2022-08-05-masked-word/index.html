*Masked Word*

#CAS  #MFT  #Tim Holtz Distress Inks  #A Colorful Life Designs  #My Favorite Things 
August 5, 2022

I tried something a little different (at least for me) with this card… nothing earth shattering, but I masked off the word celebrate before I ink blended over a stencil.  Before I removed my mask, I also blended a little more of the same colours of ink over the piece after removing the stencil to soften the look of the colours a little and to really make my word pop.  I like how it all looks kind of soft and dreamy.

Card Info

*Ink:  Tim Holtz Twisted Citron, Salty Oean

*Dies:  MFT Celebrate

*Stencil:  A Colorful Life Designs Dot Duo

*Embellishments:  unknown bling

*Etc:  foam adhesive
