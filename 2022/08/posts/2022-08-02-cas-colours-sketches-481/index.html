*CAS Colours & Sketches #481*

#CC&S  #Design Team  #CAS  #Stampin' Up!  #CAS Colours & Sketches  #Taylored Expressions  #Spellbinders 
August 2, 2022

Happy Tuesday creative friends!  It’s time for a new challenge with the team at CAS Colours & Sketches… and for the month of August, I am the host!  We are staring off this month with a colour challenge.  I can’t wait to see what everyone creates using these great colours!  Be sure to check out the CAS Colours & Sketches blog to see what the rest of the design team created!

I went for a graphic use of these colours… and these Spellbinders Colorblock dies are awesome for this!  I simply cut out the hexagon once on each colour of cardstock and layered them together to get three different hexagons.   As always I love the look of a thin dark mat to create a frame to draw your eye towards the design.

Card Info

*Cardstock:  Stampin’ Up! Balmy Blue, Highland Heather, Starry Sky

*Images:  Taylored Expressions Simple Strips

*Ink:  Versafine Clair Nocturne

*Dies:  Taylored Expressions Simple Strips; Spellbinders Colorblock Hexagons

*Tools:  Perfect Layers; Fricken Square Corners

*Etc:  foam adhesive

*Challenges:  CAS Colours & Sketches #481
