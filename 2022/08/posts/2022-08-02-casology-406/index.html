#Design Team  #CAS  #Taylored Expressions  #CASology  #Studio Katia 
August 2, 2022

It’s time for a new challenge at CASology and the cue for this month is SAND. I hope you’ll check out what the rest of the design team has created and maybe even share a creation of your own!!  You’ll find the CASology blog HERE

Despite loving the beach, I don’t have a lot of a things that are beach themed… so I had to think about how to create something with this cue.   I decided to “sand” the raised parts off of an embossed piece… but how to make it clean and simple?  As you can see from the photo, I placed a circle in my embossing folder to create a small embossed element instead of an entire background. Foil cardstock is great for this technique because the white core of the paper shows when you sand it.
