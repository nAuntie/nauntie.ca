*Circle in the Square*

#Crafty Meraki  #CAS  #Pink Fresh Studio  #Waffle Flower  #Uniko 
August 3, 2022

I don’t make a lot of square cards…. there’s nothing wrong with them, they look great, but I just don’t stray from my usual size very often.  But for some reason, when I was designing this card, it wasn’t working on a regular A2 card so I changed things up and LOVE how it looks on the square!  I added two extra pieces from the Pink Fresh Studio Rainbow Floral Washi tape to the wreath for a little something “extra”.

Card Info

*Images:  Uniko Wonderful Day

*Ink:  Versafine Clair Nocturne

*Dies:  Pink Fresh Studio Rainbow Floral; Waffle Flower Additional Circles

*Etc:  Pink Fresh Studio Rainbow Floral washi tape; Crafty Meraki gems
