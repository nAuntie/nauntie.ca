*AAACards #221*

#Design Team  #CAS  #MFT  #AAA Cards  #Copics  #AAA  #My Favorite Things  #Studio Katia 
August 7, 2022

It’s time for the first clean and simple challenge of August with AAACards.  Our challenge for the next two weeks is “small and few” which means you should use a few small images on your card… the optional twist is (lady)bugs.  The design team has some wonderful inspiration on the blog.  We hope to see you playing along with us!

I love ladybugs and they are so easy to colour for someone like me who doesn’t find colouring zen-inducing.  I thought I had a set of dies that cut ladybugs but I couldn’t find them so I went with these little guys from a stamp set by MFT.  I drew a faint pencil line to get the shape I wanted and then added the dots with a black marker. I waited about an hour before I erased my pencil line because the last thing I wanted to to was smudge that blank ink!

Card Info

*Ink:  Memento Tuxedo Black; Versafine Clair Nocturne; Copics; black pen
