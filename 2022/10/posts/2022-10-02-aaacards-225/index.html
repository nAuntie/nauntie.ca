*AAACards #225*

#Design Team  #MFT  #AAA Cards  #Christmas  #Copics  #AAA  #My Favorite Things  #Simon Says Stamp 
October 2, 2022

It’s a 1 of 9 rectangles challenge at AAACards with the optional twist of gnomes… We’d love to see your cards where you use only one of the 9 rectangles found if you divide a rectangular card in 9ths.  Be sure to keep it clean and simple and share it with us HERE!

You KNEW I’d have to use the optional twist!  I used a non-Christmas gnome stamp set but added a tiny holiday wish to it to make it a Christmas card.
