*CAS Colours & Sketches #490*

#CC&S  #Design Team  #CAS  #Stampin' Up!  #CAS Colours & Sketches  #AlteNew  #PerfectLayers(We R Memory Keepers Layers Guides) 
October 4, 2022

Hello again, crafty friends!  It’s time for a new colour challenge at CAS Colours and Sketches.  I can’t wait to see how you all create with the colours below!

I’ve said it before… I struggled with these three colours.  This was a new level of struggle for me.  If I wasn’t on the design team, I probably would have passed it up.  I just could not do anything that I liked with these colours.  I tossed several ideas.  This was the best of what I came up with and while it’s OK, it’s definitely not my most favourite card.  Be sure to check out what the rest of the design team created because there are some beauties on the CC&S Blog!!

Card Info

*Cardstock:  Stampin’ Up! Pumpkin Pie, Daffodil Delight, Shaded Spruce

*Images:  Altenew Sincere Greetings; Stampin’ Up! Sweet Songbirds
