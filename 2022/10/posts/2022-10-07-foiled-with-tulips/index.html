*Foiled with Tulips*

#Crafty Meraki  #CAS  #Hot Foil  #Spellbinders Hot Foil Glimmer Machine  #PerfectLayers(We R Memory Keepers Layers Guides) 
October 7, 2022

I’m not honestly sure about this card… it looks better in person than it does in this photo, but I’m still not sure about it.  I had these white tulips cut out from a different project and had originally thought I’d blend ink on them.  I had them laid out on a white piece of paper and I kind of liked how they looked so I went with it.

Card Info

*Dies:  Crafty Meraki Tulips; MFT Stitched Rectangles

*Foil Plate:  Spellbinders Let Your Sentiments Shine

*Etc:  Spellbinders Rainbow Foil
