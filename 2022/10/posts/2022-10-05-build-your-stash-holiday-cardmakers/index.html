*Build Your Stash Holiday Cardmakers*

#Design Team  #Lisa Horton Crafts  #Stampin' Up!  #Build Your Stash  #Christmas  #BYSHC  #Simon Says Stamp 
October 5, 2022

I am very excited to announce that I have joined the design team at the Build Your Stash Holiday Cardmakers blog!!  This is an awesome sketch challenge blog that has a new sketch each month, encouraging you to create Christmas cards each month.  I will be sharing two cards each month with the monthly sketch.  I hope you’ll check them out and join in the fun!

This is the sketch for October…  I LOVE that they include measurements!!!

I honestly had planned on putting something on top of my top layer… but this embossed pine cone and for limb was too perfect on it’s own.  I just couldn’t cover it up!
