*Gold and Black Wishes*

#CAS  #Hot Foil  #Spellbinders Hot Foil Glimmer Machine  #Waffle Flower  #Studio Katia 
October 3, 2022

I am loving my new Glimmer Foil Machine and I just know I’m going to use it a lot during my Christmas card making season (which I think will begin very shortly!).  The plate I used for this card is supposed to go across the card to create “lights” but I liked how it gave a neat background to play with going vertically instead.

Card Info

*Cardstock:  Stampin’ Up! Basic Black

*Images:  Studio Katia Birthday Greetings

*Ink:  Versafine Clair Nocturne

*Dies:  Waffle Flower Additional Circles

*Foil Plates:  Spellbinders Glimmer String Lights Background; Pink Fresh Studio solid foil plate

*Foil:  Spellbinders gold
