*CASology #408*

#Design Team  #CAS  #Lawn Fawn  #Picket Fence Studios  #Christmas  #CASology  #Simon Says Stamp  #Waffle Flower 
October 4, 2022

It is with some sadness that I am here with the final challenge at CASology... after many years of wonderful clean and simple challenges, Melissa has decided it is time to retire the CASology Challenge.  I think it would be an awesome send off to have a lot of cards submitted for this final challenge… an awesome tribute to a great challenge and to a great team of inspiring CAS creators.  I sure hope you’ll share your CAS card with the cue of cozy on the CASology Blog!

I chose to create my favourite type of card for this challenge…  a Christmas Card… with cozy wishes…

Card Info

*Cardstock:  Lawn Fawn holographic silver

*Images:  Simon Says Stamp Warmest Wishes

*Ink:  Versafine Clair Nocturne

*Dies:  Picket Fence Studio Lydia Snowflake; Waffle Flower Additional Circles

*Challenges:  CASology #408

Thanks for so many wonderful challenges CASology!  I will miss you!
