*Rainbow Striped Corners*

#CAS  #MFT  #My Favorite Things  #PerfectLayers(We R Memory Keepers Layers Guides)  #Simon Says Stamp 
September 30, 2022

How on earth is it the end of September already?  I’m still struggling to write the word September at work and now, tomorrow, I have to write October?!  We’ve had a great month here weatherwise and so far haven’t seen any snow fly (we often get the first snow in September).  We actually hit 28C (82F) earlier this week which is unheard of at this time of year!  I worry that winter will be a lot less kind than fall has been so far!

I finally found a bit of mojo the other day and was able to create some cards!  I am off work today and am hoping that Mr Mojo is at my craft desk waiting for me again!

Card Info

*Cardstock:  Stampin’ Up! Granny Apple Green

*Paper:  MFT Birthday Brights

*Images:  MFT Essential Sentiments

*Ink:  Versafine Clair Nocturne

*Dies:  Simon Says Stamps Big Happy
