*Free Form Frame*

#CAS  #Tim Holtz Distress Inks  #Pink Fresh Studio  #Studio Katia 
September 29, 2022

Another card from the bin of extras….  

I used a die as a guide to draw a rectangle on my card front with pencil… and then went over it a few times with a thin black marker, not even trying to make straight lines.  Once it had set for a bit I erased the pencil lines and then added in the hash marks and dots.  I stamped my sentiment and added to my ink blended butterfly.

To create a little dimension, I added glue along the back of the butterfly body and foam adhesive to the wings.  I held the body portion against the cardstock until it dried to ensure it had a good stick.
