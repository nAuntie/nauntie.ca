I absolutely love the sketch this week at CAS Colours & Sketches!  My card went together pretty quickly once I decided on what elements I wanted to use.  I hope you’ll play along with us!

My card uses a few pieces from my bin of extras that I kept overlooking.  I had used just two of the layering stencils and these two pieces had very little colour on them… so I grabbed my Copics and gave them a bit more colour before adding them to my card. This could be a great sketch for Christmas as well (jingle bells, snowflakes, mittens).
