*Less is More #474*

#LessIsMore  #Design Team  #LIM  #CAS  #Scrapbook.com  #MFT  #Pink Fresh Studio  #My Favorite Things 
September 24, 2022

October 1st is World Cardmaking Day and here at Less is More, we are celebrating a little early with a friendship themed challenge!  We think making a card for a friend is a great way to celebrate World Cardmaking Day!  We hope you’ll play along!

I’ve got a couple of very good friends who are always there for me… to listen, to provide support, to make me laugh, to distract me, to do social things with… so I thought I’d make a card that I could give to one of them.  I don’t have a lot of close friends but I am very lucky to have two very good friends that I know are always in my corner (and I am always in theirs too!)

Card Info

*Cardstock:  Stampin’ Up! Basic Black; gold mirror

*Images:  MFT Anything But Basic Friendship

*Ink:  Versafine Clair Nocturne

*Dies:  MFT Friend; Scrapbook.com Nested Hexagons; Pink Fresh Studio Rainbow Floral
