*Subdued Thank You*

#Fricken Square Corners  #Catherine Pooler  #CAS  #Picket Fence Studios  #Perfect Layers  #AlteNew 
September 5, 2022

Back in early July we lost a very good friend after a short illness… it was devastating to lose a friend that we loved so much.  She was one of my regular customers in terms of card orders.  Her husband approached me after her funeral, asking if I would please make him some thank you cards to send out to people who helped out so much during her brief illness and after her death… and I said “of course”.

He had no guidelines for me, but I knew what I wanted to do…. I wanted something subdued and something floral as Steph was a master gardener.  This was the card I came up with.
