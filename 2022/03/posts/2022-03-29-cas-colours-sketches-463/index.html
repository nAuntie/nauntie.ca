It’s a Tuesday, so it’s time for a new challenge with CAS Colours and Sketches.  As it is the fifth Tuesday of a month, our challenge is a double… Jeanne has chosen a past sketch and some retired colours to play with this week!  Please make sure to have these three colours as the prominent colours of your clean and simple creation (but you can add neutrals as well).  You are welcome to flip or rotate the sketch as long as the essence of the sketch remains.

I didn’t have any Watermelon Wonder so I had to improvise with my stash… and since I don’t think it quite matches, I made it the smallest amount of colour I could LOL
