*Beautiful Blooms Birthday*

#Fricken Square Corners  #CAS  #Copics  #Pink Fresh Studio  #PerfectLayers(We R Memory Keepers Layers Guides)

A few weeks ago my friend Bonnie and I went to one of our favourite local stamp stores after I was done work.  I will admit I bought a few things I didn’t need… but I am so happy I did!  This fabulous floral set from Pink Fresh is perfect for my colouring preferences… You’ll see a few cards using this set in the next several days as I stamped them up at once and spent some time colouring.
