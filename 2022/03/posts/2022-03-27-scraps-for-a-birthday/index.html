*Scraps for a Birthday*

#CAS  #Stampin' Up!  #Taylored Expressions 
March 27, 2022

I reached in to the bin of extras for the card today…  a little strip of patterned paper, an even smaller strip of cardstock and some flowers I’d cut out for something else.  I love it when these cards can come together like this.

That strip of bright green cardstock was literally in my recycling bin….. it was a long skinny strip that I trimmed off for another card and I just made it the right length for the front of the card.
