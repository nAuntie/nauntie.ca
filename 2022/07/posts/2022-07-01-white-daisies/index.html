*White Daisies*

#CAS  #A Jillian Vance Design  #Studio Katia 
July 1, 2022

How on earth is it July already?!?!?!   I wish the warm months could slow down just a little bit….  I love the extra sunlight and warmth (especially compared to the cold and dark of winter).

I want you to click on this card so you can zoom in a little bit because no matter how hard I tried, I couldn’t get my photo to look good at a small size.  I LOVE how this card turned out and I just wish it looked better in a photograph.  I got a new phone and sometimes the photos aren’t quite what I want them to be.
