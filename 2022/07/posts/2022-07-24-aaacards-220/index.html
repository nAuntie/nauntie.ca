*AAACards #220*

#Catherine Pooler  #Design Team  #Poppy Stamps  #CAS  #Tim Holtz Distress Inks  #AAA Cards  #AAA 
July 24, 2022

It’s time for a new clean and simple challenge at AAA Cards!  We want to see your cards with the design only along the edge (you can choose which edge) with the optional twist of “in the sea”.

This was tough for me because I had two design team obligations that were a similar theme back to back!  And the themes aren’t something I find easy to do in the first place!

For my card I ripped a piece of Post It Tape and ink blended my ocean before splattering it with water to create some interest.  I added a shark fin and a sentiment and called it done.
