#Stampin' Up!  #Christmas  #Penguin Palace  #Christmas Craft Creations  #PerfectLayers(We R Memory Keepers Layers Guides) 
July 27, 2022

There’s still time to play along with the July theme of Patterned Paper at Christmas Craft Creations.  This is a monthly challenge that helps you get at least one Christmas card made every month.  The design team always has such wonderful creations… you should check them out!

I got this one sheet of patterned paper at a garage sale and I honestly don’t know why I bought it.  I don’t scrapbook and honestly the design on it is really not suited to cutting down for Christmas cards.  But I found a section that would work and I went for it….  the patterned paper is the majority of my card.  I added a die cut banner, a stamped sentiment and a few snowflakes to finish it off.
