#Design Team  #CAS  #MFT  #Picket Fence Studios  #CASology  #My Favorite Things  #Waffle Flower 
July 5, 2022

It’s time for a new challenge at CASology... our cue for you for July is WINDY.

I have a funny wind story… last fall we put up some security cameras over our garage.  Since the trees have leafed this year, we often get alerts from the cameras of “motion”… the other day we had hundreds of alerts in the morning when we work up… each one of them was the leaves on the trees thanks to the wind!

I went with the birthday candle idea for my windy card…  as I approach 52 later this month I’ll have to have a lot of “windiness” to get all those candles blown out!
