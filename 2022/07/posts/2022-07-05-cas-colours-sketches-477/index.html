*CAS Colours & Sketches #477*

#CC&S  #Design Team  #CAS  #MFT  #CAS Colours & Sketches  #Copics  #Perfect Layers  #Brother Scan n Cut  #My Favorite Things  #Paper Rose 
July 5, 2022

Patricia is our host for the month of July at CAS Colours & Sketches… she chose this wonderfully right colour palette for our first challenge this month.

Being a part of this design team, I have discovered that I am much better at sketch challenges than I am with colour challenges.  When I was only playing along, if the colours didn’t speak to me, I didn’t play.  If the colours were at all challenging to me, I didn’t play.  But now I *have* to play each week and it’s hard sometimes!

This was one of those weeks!

Card Info
