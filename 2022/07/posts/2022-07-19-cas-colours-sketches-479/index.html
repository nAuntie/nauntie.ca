*CAS Colours & Sketches #479*

#CC&S  #Design Team  #CAS  #Stampin' Up!  #CAS Colours & Sketches  #Pink Fresh Studio  #Studio Katia 
July 19, 2022

It’s time for a new colour challenge at CAS Colours & Sketches!  Two of these colours are not my favourites… one of them I honestly only have two sheets of thanks to my friend Bonnie giving them to me!  But I will admit… this is a great colour combination and I am pretty happy with how my card turned out!

As I don’t have many Stampin’ Up! ink pads any more and I struggle with trying to find suitable matches with the inks I do have, most of my colour challenges will have cardstock as the colours instead.  I think this simple design turned out pretty good!

Card Info

*Cardstock: Stampin’ Up! So Saffron, Soft Sea Foam, Blushing Bride

*Images:  Studio Katia Birthday Greetings

*Ink:  Versafine Clair Nocturne

*Dies:  Stampin’ Up! Country Wreaths, Tasteful Labels; Pink Fresh Studio Slim Stripes

*Challenges:  CAS Colours & Sketches #479
