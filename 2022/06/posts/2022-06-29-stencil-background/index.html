*Stencil Background*

#Fricken Square Corners  #CAS  #Tim Holtz Distress Inks  #GinaK  #A Colorful Life Designs  #Taylored Expressions  #PerfectLayers(We R Memory Keepers Layers Guides)  #Studio Katia 
June 29, 2022

I had fun with some light stencilling for my card today.  I wanted some pretty colour without it being overwhelming.  I love layering stencils since it’s so easy to do one colour at a time… it’s achievable with a regular stencil but you have to be careful with the masking!  I actually used two different stencils for this card but only used a small portion of each, moving it around to fill my background.

Card Info

*Cardstock:  Stampin’ Up! Basic Black

*Images:  Uniko Birthday Wishes

*Ink:  Versafine Clair Nocturne; Tim Holtz Distress Twisted Citron, Picked Raspberry

*Stencils:  Gina K Bold Flowers; A Colorful Life Designs Daisies

*Embellishments:  Studio Katia Arctic Breeze Pearls

*Tools:  Perfect Layers; Fricken’ Square Corners; Taylored Expressions blending brushes
