It’s time for a new clean and simple challenge at AAA Cards!  We have a sketch for you this week with the added optional twist of flowers.  Of course I did both (because I love florals!)  Share your card with us HERE for a chance to be chosen as a guest designer in an upcoming challenge!

I always struggle with the placement of things like the sketch shows so I wanted to do something different.  Instead of making those circles my flowers, I decided to use the idea of circles to colour my floral image.  It’s not exactly like the sketch but I think you can see the sketch in m creation.
