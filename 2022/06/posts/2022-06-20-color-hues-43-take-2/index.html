*Color Hues #43... take 2*

#Fricken Square Corners  #CAS  #MFT  #Tim Holtz Distress Inks  #ColorHues  #Pink Fresh Studio  #My Favorite Things  #PerfectLayers(We R Memory Keepers Layers Guides) 
June 20, 2022

Is it good or bad that this Pink Fresh Studio stamp is one stamp with several images that you stamp, stencil and cut all at once?  It’s good because it’s easy, it’s bad because you get stuck with several cards that look similar.

The good news is that this is a LOVELY set and it’s easy to create with it! And I get to play along with Color Hues again!

Card Info

*Cardstock:  Stampin’ Up! Basic Black

*Images:  Pink Fresh Studio Rainbow Floral; Uniko Birthday Wishes

*Ink:  Versafine Clair Nocturne; Tim Holtz Twisted Citron, Salty Ocean

*Dies:  Pink Fresh Studio Rainbow Floral; MFT Stitched Rectangles

*Tools:  Perfect Layers; Fricken Square Corners

*Etc:  foam adhesive

*Challenges:  Color Hues #43
