#Design Team  #MFT  #Tim Holtz Distress Inks  #AAA Cards  #AAA  #Pink Fresh Studio  #My Favorite Things 
June 12, 2022

Thanks for popping by today!  I’m sharing with you, my card for the current clean and simple challenge at AAA Cards.  We want you to use only 1 of the 9 rectangles on the front of your A2 card when you divide it up evenly (or 1/9 of any card size you like to use) with the optional twist of Butterflies.

The 1/9th is the hard part for this card… I decided to use a little ink blending to create the rectangle to help me envision it… to create a little dimension, I used glue along the body of the butterfly and foam adhesive on the wings to make them pop up a bit

Card Info

*Images:  Pink Fresh Studio Small Butterflies; MFT Bitty Birthday Wishes

*Ink:  Versafine Clair Nocturne; Tim Holtz Distress Ink Wilted Violet, Salty Ocean, Mustard Seed

*Etc:  foam adhesive
