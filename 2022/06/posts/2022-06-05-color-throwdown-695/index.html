*Color Throwdown #695*

#Fricken Square Corners  #Catherine Pooler  #Color Throwdown  #A Colorful Life Designs  #PerfectLayers(We R Memory Keepers Layers Guides)  #Simon Says Stamp 
June 5, 2022

I pulled out a stencil that I purchased right before I broke my wrist.  Ink blending still isn’t easy (and honestly it can hurt) but this was quick and I was able to get through it.  I loved the look of the colours in the current Color Throwdown Challenge so that’s what I went with for this card.

Card Info

*Cardstock:  Stampin’ Up! Basic Black; silver mirror

*Images:  MFT Big Birthday Sentiments

*Ink:  Versafine Clair Nocturne; Catherine Pooler Stone Blue, Serene, Hot Tub

*Dies:  Simon Says Stamp Big Happy

*Stencil:  A Colorful Life Designs Stripes Waves

*Tools:  Perfect Layers; Fricken Square Corners

*Challenges:  Color Throwdown #695
