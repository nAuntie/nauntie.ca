*Freshly Made Sketches #539

#Lawn Fawn  #MFT  #Stampin' Up!  #Pink Fresh Studio  #My Favorite Things 
June 9, 2022

I created a quick card to play along with the current sketch at Freshly Made Sketches…  I had the card all put together and ready to photograph and I didn’t like it.  So I used my stitched rectangle die to cut off the front of the card and layered it on to another card and I like it so much better.  The extra panel and the stitched lines really helped to make this card.

Card Info

*Cardstock:  Stampin’ Up! Smoky Slate, Pear Pizzazz

*Images:  Pink Fresh Studio Beautiful Blooms

*Ink:  Versafine Clair Nocturne

*Dies:  Lawn Fawn Everyday Sentiment Banners; Stampin’ Up! Splended Stems; MFT Stitched Rectangles
