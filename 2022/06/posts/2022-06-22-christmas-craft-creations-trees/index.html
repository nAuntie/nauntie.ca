There’s still some time to play along with the Christmas Craft Creations June theme of TREES!  This is a great challenge to help you get a few Christmas cards made throughout the year.  I had promised myself to make more Christmas cards before October and while I’ve done that, it’s really only been for this challenge!  Maybe I should make one day a month where I make only Christmas cards.

Anyway… I found these Spellbinders Bottlebrush Trees second hand on a Facebook group and snatched them up.  I knew I wanted to use them for this challenge.
