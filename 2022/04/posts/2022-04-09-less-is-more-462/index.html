It’s time for a new challenge with the team at Less is More.  As you can see from the graphic below, the prompt is “occupations”.  As always, we hope you’ll play along with our challenge and share your creations with us HERE!

I struggled a bit with the challenge as I don’t really have any stamps that scream “occupations” to me… so I had to think outside the box a little.  We all know I love to create with floral stamps so I decided to be an artist as my occupation….  I stamped the daisies image and then used some ink blot stamps to “colour” in the image… an artistic take.

And you know what? I like how it turned out!
