*Less is More #463*

#LessIsMore  #Design Team  #LIM  #CAS  #MFT  #Tim Holtz Distress Inks  #AlteNew 
April 23, 2022

I’m really missing my creative space now… I had surgery on my right wrist yesterday and am hoping things will start to feel better again soon.  I’m only sharing design team creations now as typing blog posts is very slow with only one hand.

It’s Saturday so it’s time for another challenge with the team ay Simply Less is More…

I focussed my one layer card on the bottom of my card. tending towards the right.  I masked off an area, stamped and embossed the sentiment and stamped the floral.  I then reverse masked and used Distress Inks and finger daubers to create the rainbow.

I hope you’ll play along!
