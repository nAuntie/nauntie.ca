#Design Team  #Avery Elle  #CAS  #Lawn Fawn  #AAA Cards  #Copics  #AAA 
April 17, 2022

It’s time for another clean and simple challenge with the team at AAACards.  Our challenge for the next two weeks is “small and few” with the optional twist of Easter or Spring.  I hope you’ll play along with us by sharing your clean and simple creations with us HERE!

I did a quick and easy card thanks to this wonderful Lawn Fawn strip of flowers.  I stamped them, coloured them quickly with a few spring colours of Copics, stamped my greeting and then put the flowers on the card with some foam adhesive.
