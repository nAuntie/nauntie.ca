*Simple Sympathy*

#Fricken Square Corners  #CAS  #Stampin' Up!  #Pink Fresh Studio  #Taylored Expressions 
May 20, 2022

I have guidelines (for myself, not for others) as to what a sympathy card should look like.  I don’t judge what others create, but I do like to keep the cards I create simple and “toned down”.  I needed a card for friends who recently suffered a loss and managed to create this card using some extras that were in my dish.

Card Info

*Cardstock:  Stampin’ Up! Old Olive

*Images:  Pink Fresh Studio Small Butterflies; Taylored Expressions Simple Strips

*Ink:  Versamark; Versafine Clair Nocturne

*Dies:  Stampin’ Up! Layering Ovals; MFT Stitched Rectangles; Taylored Expressions Simple Strips; Pink Fresh Studio Small Butterflies

*Etc:  WOW Opaque Bright White embossing powder

*Tools:  Fricken Square Corners
