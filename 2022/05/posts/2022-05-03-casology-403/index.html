Happy Tuesday creative friends… I saw my surgeon yesterday and as expected, the plaster splint was removed and I now have a removable brace.  I can wear the brace when I’m up and about or if the arm/wrist is fatigued.  I have some stretches thatI’ve started doing that are really tough but I am told it will all get easier… I am to use my arm and wrist as tolerated for anything non-weight bearing.  I will start some physical therapy in a few weeks to help me regain motion and strength.  It will be about another 4 weeks before I start looking at a gradual return to work.

Anyway… it’s time for a new challenge with CASology… our cue word for this month is “botanical”

If you’ve been around a while, you know I love creating with florals, so this was not a difficult challenge for me!

I love the look of a white on white layer… but I do like to step it up a bit by adding foam adhesive for some dimension.

I hope you will play along with us by sharing your creation HERE.
