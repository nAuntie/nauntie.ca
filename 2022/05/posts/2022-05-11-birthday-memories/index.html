*Birthday Memories*

#CAS  #Tim Holtz Distress Inks  #Stampin' Up!  #Uniko 
May 11, 2022

Hello crafty friends.  I have been crafting a little bit as I find it is good therapy. both mentally and physically.  I am also working here and there without my splint on (which I’m supposed to do) and while I’m not typing as fast as I normally would, it’s not the one handed hunt and peck I’ve been doing in the last month.  With things working a little more in my favour, I’ve decided to blog more often again … I still have cards to share that I created before my fall.

I played with some ink blending and created a few different panels of the same colours, working on different cardstocks to see how they would blend differently.  I wasn’t sure about these colours together at first but I really like how they turned out.
