*Awesome Daisy Birthday*

#A Jillian Vance Design  #Stampin' Up!  #Studio Katia 
May 25, 2022

I can’t believe we’re looking at the end of May already… next week we’ll be in to June!  It doesn’t help that the weather hasn’t been consistently warm here in Calgary so I haven’t switched the furnace from heat to cool.  It’s sad, really.

I really love the look of this pretty blue with the white flowers on top… I added a little yellow to make it all pop!
