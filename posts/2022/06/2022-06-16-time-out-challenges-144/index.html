It’s time for a new challenge at Time Out Challenges… up for grabs this week is a $25 credit at Top Flight Stamps!  Play along with us by sharing your card HERE.  Our prompt for this challenge is “tropical”.

I had some fun with the new Stampin’ Up! Colours and an old stamp and die set.  Some of the leaves are stamped, some are cut out… I was able to create a little more dimension this way.
