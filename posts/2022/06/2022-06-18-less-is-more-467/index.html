#Catherine Pooler  #LessIsMore  #Design Team  #LIM  #CAS  #GinaK  #AlteNew 
June 18, 2022

There’s a new one layer card challenge over at Simply Less is More…  a one layer card means there are no other pieces of cardstock other than your card base. If you want to use dies, they have to be inlaid.  For me, the best way to achieve a one layer card is some simple stamping.

*you may add words as well*

I had a thought in my head but had trouble getting all of the pieces together.  A die cut would have been so much easier LOL!  I found a two step stamping leaf stamp in my stash and instead of trying to find two colours of ink, I stamped off the ink of scrap before stamping the inside of the leaves to create a new colour.
