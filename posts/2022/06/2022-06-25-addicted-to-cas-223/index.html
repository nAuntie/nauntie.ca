*Addicted to CAS #223*

#AddictedtoCAS  #Design Team  #CAS  #Scrapbook.com  #Stampin' Up!  #Pink Fresh Studio  #Taylored Expressions  #ATCAS 
June 25, 2022

Good morning crafty friends… it’s time for a new challenge with Addicted to CAS!  We are playing with the code word GRID for this challenge.  

I will admit that I struggled with this challenge.  I had no idea how to interpret this word.  But then I remembered I had some patterned papers that had a grid-look to them… and I went with the one that was more delicate looking to create my card for today.

Card Info

  • Cardstock:  Stampin’ Up! Basic Black; gold mirror

  • Paper:  Stampin’ Up! Well Suited

  • Images:  Taylored Expressions Simple Strips Birthday

  • Ink:  Versafine Clair Nocturne

  • Dies:  Scrapbook.com Nested Hexagons; Pink Fresh Studio Rainbow Floral; Taylored Expressions Simple Strips, Simple Strips Backer

  • Etc:  Pink Fresh Studio Rainbow Floral washi tape

  • Tools:  Perfect Layers
