*The Best Day Ever*

#Fricken Square Corners  #CAS  #MFT  #Stampin' Up!  #Taylored Expressions  #PerfectLayers(We R Memory Keepers Layers Guides) 
June 15, 2022

I worked on this card a few weeks ago while my wrist was still pretty sore and useless (instead of kind of sore and useless like it is now) and decided to play with a design that featured patterned paper.  Doing this made it all a little easier for me and my wrist but still allowed for me to feel like I was being creative.

Card Info

  • Cardstock:  Stampin’ Up! Night of Navy

  • Paper:  Stampin’ Up! Paper Blooms

  • Images:  Taylored Expressions Simple Strips

  • Ink:  Versasfine Clair Nocturne

  • Dies:  Taylored Expressions Simple Strips; MFT Stitched Rectangles

  • Tools:  Perfect Layers; Fricken Square Corners\
