I am so happy that Samantha chose some of the new Stampin’ Up! In Color colours for our challenge at CAS Colours & Sketches this week.  I just love the new colours and especially these three!  I really hope you’ll play along with this colour combination this week!

These colours are just so happy!  They make me think of popsicles, and beach balls, and shovels and pails… all those wonderfully fun things in the summer!
