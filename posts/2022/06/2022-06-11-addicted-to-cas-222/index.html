*Addicted to CAS #222*

#AddictedtoCAS  #Design Team  #Stampin' Up!  #PerfectLayers(We R Memory Keepers Layers Guides)  #ATCAS 
June 11, 2022

It’s time for a new challenge at Addicted to CAS… the code word for challenge #222 is DOTS!  There are so many wonderfully fun ideas on the challenge blog by the design team… I hope you’ll go check them out.  And then you need to create your own dotted masterpieces to share with us!  

I wanted to have some fun with the new Stampin’ Up! In Color colours for 2022-2024… so I used some old circle punches and cut out a bunch in three different colours.  I used a Swiss Dots embossing folder for a little more fun and interest.

Card Info

*Cardstock:  Stampin’ Up! Parakeet Party, Soft Sorbet, Tahitian Tide

*Images:  Taylored Expressions Simple Strips Birthday

*Ink:  Versafine Clair Nocturne

*Dies:  Taylored Expressions Simple Strips

*Tools:  Swiss Dots embossing folder; assorted sizes of circle punches; Perfect Layers

*Challenges:  Addicted to CAS #222
