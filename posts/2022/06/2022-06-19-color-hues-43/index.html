*Color Hues #43*

#Fricken Square Corners  #CAS  #Tim Holtz Distress Inks  #ColorHues  #GinaK  #Pink Fresh Studio  #PerfectLayers(We R Memory Keepers Layers Guides) 
June 19, 2022

I’ve been struggling in the craft room in the last few days… I sit there, wanting to create but nothing comes.  Thankfully I have some cards already made to be able to share with you… and it just so happens to fall in to the color challenge at Color Hues!

For the card today, I used the Pink Fresh Studio Rainbow Floral stamp, die and stencils… but instead of using all of the layers of the stencil, I only used two to create the bit of blue on the flowers and the bit of green on the leaves.  I really like this look (and use it all the time with the Altenew layering stamp sets I own).

Card Info

  • Cardstock:  Stampin’ Up! Basic Black

  • Images:  Pink Fresh Studio Rainbow Floral; Gina K Where Flowers Bloom

  • Ink:  Versafine Clair Nocturne; Tim Holtz Twisted Citron, Salty Ocean

  • Dies:  Pink Fresh Studio Rainbow Floral

  • Tools:  Perfect Layers; Fricken Square Corners

  • Etc:  foam adhesive

  • Challenges:  Color Hues #43
