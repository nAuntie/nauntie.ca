It’s time for a new clean and simple challenge at Less is More!  We want to see your “masculine” themed cards for the next two weeks!  Share them with us HERE!!!

I always struggle with masculine cards.  I don’t have much for masculine images and I tend towards purples and reds in my colour selections.  I will admit, I almost skipped this challenge!  I was poking around Pinterest and searched for “masculine colour combinations” and something similar to what I used popped up.   I figured I could find a way to use those colours in a more graphic card and then remember this Stampin’ Up! set I bought but hadn’t really used.  In the end, while I don’t LOVE this card, I do think it’s pretty good.

Card Info

  • Cardstock:  Stampin’ Up! Misty Moonlight

  • Images:  Stampin’ Up! Biggest Wish

  • Ink:  Catherine Pooler Minted, Aquatini, Sauna, Juniper Mist

  • Dies:  MFT Stitched Rectangles

  • Tools:  Perfect Layers; Fricken Square Corners

  • Challenges:  Less is More #466
