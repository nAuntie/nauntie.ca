*May All Your Wishes*

#CAS  #Pink Fresh Studio  #Taylored Expressions 
April 1, 2022

This is another card that I created after stamping up these wonderful floral images by Pink Fresh Studio.  After I had finished the card, I decided it needed “something” so I pulled out a black marker to create the scattered dots around the image… I have stamps that do this however I chose to do it after everything was already put together.

Card Info

  • Images:  Pink Fresh Studio Beautiful Blooms; Taylored Expression Simple Strips Birthday

  • Ink:  Memento Tuxedo Black; Copics

  • Dies:  Pink Fresh Studio Beautiful Blooms; Taylored Expressions Simple Strips

  • Embellishments:  black marker; Nuvo Aqua Shimmer

  • Etc:  foam adhesive
