*CASology #402*

#Catherine Pooler  #Design Team  #CAS  #Lawn Fawn  #Pink Fresh Studio  #Taylored Expressions  #CASology 
April 5, 2022

I’m back with the design team at CASology for our April challenge.  There are a lot of wonderful cards by the design team to see, so I hope you’ll head to the blog to see them!  We’d love to see what you create as well!  Share your creations with us HERE!

When I saw the cue card for this challenge, my mind automatically went to animals….  but honestly, I’ve made a lot of cards with animals on them in the last little bit and I was a little tired of them.  So I had to put on my thinking cap and find something else that could be fluffy.  The hard part was finding something else that is fluffy that I have in my stash!  I almost gave up… but then I thought about this cloud stencil I bought a while back…
