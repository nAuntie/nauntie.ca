*CAS Colours & Sketches #464*

#Fricken Square Corners  #Catherine Pooler  #CC&S  #Design Team  #CAS  #CAS Colours & Sketches  #GinaK  #Taylored Expressions  #PerfectLayers(We R Memory Keepers Layers Guides) 
April 5, 2022

Bhavya is the host at CAS Colours & Sketches for the month of April. She has chosen a great ‘garden’ themed set of colours for us to use as her first colour challenge this month.  I can’t wait to see how others interpret these colours!

For our colour challenges, you must use all of the stated challenge colours. You may also use neutral colours; however, the challenge colours should be the most noticeable ones featured on your card. We use Stampin’ Up colour names for reference, but you are welcome to use any other companies’ products as long as you match the challenge colours as closely as possible. 

I don’t have much for Stampin’ Up! inks (I have most of their cardstocks though) so I had to get creative with my colour choices for this challenge.  I used a Gina K layering stencil with some Post It Tape to create my design down the edge of my card panel.

Card Info

*Cardstock:  Stampin’ Up! Garden Green

*Images:  Gina K Crazy Daisy

*Ink:  Versafine Clair Nocturne; Catherine Pooler Hot Tub, Be Mine

*Stencils:  Gina K Bold Flowers

*Tools:  Taylored Expressions Blending Brushes; Perfect Layers; Fricken Square Corners
