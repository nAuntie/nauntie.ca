*CAS Colours & Sketches #465*

#CC&S  #Design Team  #CAS  #Tim Holtz Distress Inks  #CAS Colours & Sketches  #Pink Fresh Studio  #Studio Katia 
April 12, 2022

Happy Tuesday crafty friends… the team at CAS Colours & Sketches is back with another sketch challenge to inspire you.  We really hope you’ll play along and share your creations with us HERE!

I stamped up a sheet of those wonderful Pink Fresh Studio butterflies and embossed them in white this time.  I used a finger dauber to add colour to this butterfly after I’d cut it out, trying to keep colours in specific areas… it helped that I used similar colours.  My rectangle element I created by masking off a rectangle on the front of my card and blending Twisted Citron directly on to the card front.

Card Info

  • Stamps:  Pink Fresh Studio Small Butterflies; Studio Katia Birthday Greetings

  • Ink:  Versafine Clair Nocturne; Tim Holtz Twisted Citron, Picked Raspberry, Wilted Violet; Versamark

  • Dies:  Pink Fresh Studio Small Butterflies

  • Etc:  WOW opaque white embossing powder; finger daubers

  • Challenges:  CAS Colours & Sketches #465
