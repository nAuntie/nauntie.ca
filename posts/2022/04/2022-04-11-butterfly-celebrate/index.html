*Butterfly Celebrate*

#Pink & Main  #CAS  #Lawn Fawn  #Tim Holtz Distress Inks  #Pink Fresh Studio  #Studio Katia 
April 11, 2022

Some more love to Pink Fresh Studio and their products… but lucky for you, I’ve switched to butterflies from flowers LOL!  This is a great stamp that stamps up about a dozen butterflies all at once and the die cuts them all out at once as well.  I stamped up a sheet of them with black ink and then blended some Tim Holtz inks over the top before cutting them all out.  I saw a great mini slimline card with a rainbow of images up the centre and I decided to do one with my butterflies.  I added some diagonal embossed lines on the front panel and put it all together with a simple sentiment and a few gems.

Card Info

*Images:  Pink Fresh Studio Small Butterflies; Studio Katia Birthday Greetings

*Ink:  Versafine Clair Nocturne; Tim Holtz Distress Picked Raspberry, Carved Pumpkin, Mustard Seed, Twisted Citron, Salty Ocean, Wilted Violet

*Dies:  Pink Fresh Studio Small Butterflies;  Lawn Fawn Everyday Sentiment Banners

*Tools:  Fricken Square Corners; ScorPal

*Embellishments:  Pink & Main Rainbow Pearls

*Etc:  foam adhesive
