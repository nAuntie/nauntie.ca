There’s still time to play along with the Christmas Craft Creations April challenge!  We’d love to see your Christmas cards using plaid or tartan!

When plaid first started being popular (again), I resisted it.  I honestly wasn’t a big fan.  But over the years it’s grown on me!  There’s just something about a good old red and black buffalo check….

I had fun with some Stampin’ Up plaid papers and the L’il Gnome from Simon Says Stamp

I really hope you’ll play along with us!!
