Happy Tuesday my friends… and a BIG happy birthday to my big sister!  It’s a milestone birthday for her today and I’m sad to not be celebrating it with her.  

It’s time for a new sketch challenge with CAS Colours & Sketches.  Patricia came up with this fun sketch for us all to try this week!  I really look forward to seeing what *you* create!

I tried a few different things with this sketch and tossed them all in the bin… until I decided to turn the sketch and then I came up with something I liked on my first try.  My colours make this card eligible for the current challenge at Paper Players too!
