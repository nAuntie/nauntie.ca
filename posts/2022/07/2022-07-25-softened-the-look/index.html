*Softened the Look*

#Fricken Square Corners  #Catherine Pooler  #A Colorful Life Designs  #PerfectLayers(We R Memory Keepers Layers Guides)  #Studio Katia 
July 25, 2022

I will admit that I love stencils… and I have a few dozen… but I hardly use them because I feel intimidated by them.  I think it’s because I prefer the clean and simple style for my creations and stencils tend to be pretty busy.  I’ve been trying to pull them out to play with a little more often…  For this card I used the ink through the stencil and then the same colours of inks over top without the stencil to soften the look a little bit.  This is one of the computer generated sentiments I printed with my laser printer and foiled.  

Card Info

*Cardstock:  Stampin’ Up! Basic Black

*Stencils:  A Colorful Life Artistic Bullseye
*Ink:  Catheine Pooler Be Mine, Tiki Torch, Limoncello

*Embellishments:  Studio Katia

*Tools:  Perfect Layers; Fricken Square Corners
