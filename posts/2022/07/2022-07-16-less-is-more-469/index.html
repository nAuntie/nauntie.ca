There’s a colour challenge at Simply Less is More… and that challenge is traditional Christmas.  I hope you’ll dig out some of your Christmas stash and play along with us!

To me, the traditional colours of Christmas are red, green and gold…. and I think “sparkle” should be a Christmas colour as well LOL!  I just love sparkly things, especially for Christmas cards!

  • Images:  Simon Say Stamp Good Cheer

  • Dies:  MFT Holly Berries

  • Tools:  Perfect Layers; Fricken Square Corners
