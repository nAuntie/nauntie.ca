*Color Throwdown #699*

#Fricken Square Corners  #Nuvo Paste  #CAS  #Tim Holtz Distress Inks  #Color Throwdown  #A Colorful Life Designs  #PerfectLayers(We R Memory Keepers Layers Guides)  #Studio Katia 
July 4, 2022

I love the inspiration photo from the current Color Throwdown challenge so I had to play along.  But then I was stumped as to what I wanted to do…  I do much better with sketch challenges than colour challenges I think!

I pulled out a stencil I haven’t used yet and this is the result.  I was going to orient my card the other way however the glitter paste made a mess in one area under the stencil so I flipped the panel and covered it with my sentiment!
