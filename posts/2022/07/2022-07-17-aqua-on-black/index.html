*Aqua on Black*

#Stampin' Up!  #A Colorful Life Designs  #Simon Says Stamp  #Uniko 
July 17, 2022

This card started out as an experiment… I wanted to see what this aqua coloured paste would look like on black… and let me tell you… it looks fabulous!  It looked so nice I had to make a card with it, even if it’s not my usual clean and simple style.

Card Info

*Cardstock:  Stampin’ Up! Basic Black

*Images:  Uniko Birthday Wishes

*Ink:  Verasfine Clair Nocturne

*Dies:  Stampin’ Up! Tasteful Labels

*Stencil:  A Colorful Life Designs Merry Dots

*Etc:  Simon Says Stamps Seafoam Glitz Glitter Gel
