It’s time for a new challenge with Less is More….  our cue word for challenge #468 is “emboss”.  You can use any interpretation of the word in your clean and simple card.

I decided to go with an embossing folder for my card… and I grabbed the etched butterflies from my pile of extras from the card I shared a few days ago as I think they look embossed as well.

Card Info

  • Cardstock:  Stampin’ Up! In Color sparkle paper; gold mirror

  • Images:  MFT Bitty Birthday Wishes

  • Ink:  Versafine Clair Nocturne

  • Dies:  Paper Rose Studio Little Etched Butterflies

  • Embossing Folder:  Stampin’ Up! (retired)
