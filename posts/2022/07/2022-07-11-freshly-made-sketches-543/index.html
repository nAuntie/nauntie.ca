*Freshly Made Sketches #543*

#CAS  #Freshly Made Sketches  #Stampin' Up!  #PerfectLayers(We R Memory Keepers Layers Guides)  #Uniko 
July 11, 2022

I loved the sketch the Cecile had for Freshly Made Sketches this week… I’m glad I had a few minutes at my craft table to play along!

My card originally much more plain as I stamped directly on the larger white square and put the card together.  The more I looked at it the more I knew it needed a little something more.  I cut a smaller square and stamped on it then used my Perfect Layers tool to create a green border and then put it on top of what I had originally created… and it looks SO much better in my opinion!  It’s still not anything but clean and simple but the little pop of green made all the difference.
