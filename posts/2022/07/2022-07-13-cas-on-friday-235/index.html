*CAS on Friday #235*

#Fricken Square Corners  #CAS on Friday  #CAS  #MFT  #Copics  #My Favorite Things 
July 13, 2022

Good morning friends…  a quick post from me today to share my creations for the current CAS on Friday challenge.  The current challenge is “silhouette” and instead of stamping, I did a black die cut instead.    I used my Copics to create a little rainbow and then put the die cut floral spring on top of it.  Because I used Copics, I used a smaller panel and put it on top of a white card so that the ink bleeding wouldn’t show through to the inside of the card.
