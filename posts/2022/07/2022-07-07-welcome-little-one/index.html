*Welcome Little One*

#Catherine Pooler  #CAS  #Stampin' Up!  #Taylored Expressions 
July 7, 2022

I had a bunch of extra white flowers sitting in my bin of extras (that makes it sound huge… it’s just a Gladware sandwich sized container) and I decided to colour them up with some soft ink blending to create a little wreath of flowers to go with this new sunburst embossing folder that I purchased.

Card Info

*Images:  Taylored Expressions Simple Strips

*Ink:  Versafine Clair Nocturne; Catherine Pooler Limoncello, All That Jazz, Sixteen Candles, It’s a Girl

*Dies:  Stampin’ Up! Pierced Blooms; Taylored Expressions Simple Strips

*Etc:  Spellbinders Sun Rays embossing folder; foam adhesive
