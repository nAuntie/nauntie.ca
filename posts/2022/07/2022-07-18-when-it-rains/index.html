*When It Rains...*

#MFT  #Tim Holtz Distress Inks  #Taylored Expressions  #My Favorite Things  #PerfectLayers(We R Memory Keepers Layers Guides)  #Spellbinders 
July 18, 2022

My blog title has nothing to do with my card today… but it has to do with what’s going on in my world.  As you know, I broke my right wrist in April.  While I am getting mobility and strength back in that wrist, it would appear that the muscles around my right shoulder don’t like that I’ve been using the arm in awkward ways and they have now seized up making it near impossible to sit upright at my desk, let alone create.  This has been going on for two weeks now despite several massages and chiropractic adjustments.  I haven’t found any medications that alleviate the pain I’m in.  Just sitting here to write blog posts is difficult.

So until this gets sorted out a bit better, my blog posts may be sporadic.  I have a bit of design team stuff done for this coming week that I will post but I’m not sure how often I’ll be posting other than that.  I hope it gets sorted soon as I am really missing my creative time…

I will share a quick card for you today….
