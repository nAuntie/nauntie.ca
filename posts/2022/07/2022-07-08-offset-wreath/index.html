*Offset Wreath*

#Crafty Meraki  #CAS  #MFT  #Washi Tape  #Pink Fresh Studio  #Taylored Expressions  #My Favorite Things 
July 8, 2022

I find myself always leaning towards the same layouts when I am using a circle element on my card (and squares too) so I consciously made an effort to do something different with this rainbow floral wreath.

Card Info

*Paper:  MFT Black & White Stripes

*Images:  Taylored Expressions Simple Strips

*Ink:  Versafine Clair Nocturne

*Dies:  Pink Fresh Studio Rainbow Floral; Taylored Expressions Simple Strips

*Etc:  Pink Fresh Studio Rainbow Floral washi tape; foam adhesive

*Embellishments:  Craft Meraki Sparkly Mix
