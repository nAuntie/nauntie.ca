*Color Throwdown #706*

#CAS  #Freshly Made Sketches  #Color Throwdown  #PerfectLayers(We R Memory Keepers Layers Guides)  #Spellbinders 
August 19, 2022

I had to play along with the latest challenge at Color Throwdown because I really love the look of blue and green together.  

I usually choose the brighter forms of these colours to go together but I strayed from that with this card to use up a scrap of very old patterned paper I had in my stash.

Card Info

*Cardstock:  Stampin’ Up! Marina Mist

*Paper:  My Mind’s Eye

*Images:  Spellbinders Sentiments for Your

*Ink:  Versafine Clair Nocturne

*Tools:  Perfect Layers

*Challenges:  FMS#84; Color Throwdown #706
