*Addicted to CAS #227*

#Fricken Square Corners  #Nuvo Paste  #AddictedtoCAS  #Design Team  #CAS  #Tim Holtz Distress Inks  #A Colorful Life Designs  #PerfectLayers(We R Memory Keepers Layers Guides)  #ATCAS 
August 20, 2022

There’s a new challenge at Addicted to CAS today… time to show us your clean and simple creations featuring the colour pink!

I don’t know why but I struggled with this prompt….  but then I gabbed out some stencils and had a little play with them.  I stencilled the pink swirls first and then put a second stencil on top of the first stencil and used Glimmer Paste so that the sparkly circles only showed up on the pink swirls.

Card Info

*Cardstock:  Stampin’ Up! Basic Black

*Sentiment:  computer generated and printed with a laser printer and foiled

*Ink:  Tim Holtz Picked Raspberry

*Stencils:  A Colorful Life Designs Big Swirls, Merry Dots

*Embellishments:  Moonstone Nuvo Glimmer Paste

*Tools:  Perfect Layers; Fricken Square Corners

*Challenges:  Addicted to CAS #227
