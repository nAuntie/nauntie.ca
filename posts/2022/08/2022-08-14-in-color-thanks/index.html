*In Color Thanks*

#Fricken Square Corners  #CAS  #Stampin' Up!  #AlteNew  #PerfectLayers(We R Memory Keepers Layers Guides)  #Spellbinders 
August 14, 2022

I just love the 2022-24 Stampin’ Up! In Color colours…. but I don’t find I use all of them much.  I am really drawn to the red, green and light blue but not the two darker colours as much.  So I grabbed all five for this card.

Card Info

  • Cardstock:  Stampin’ Up! Sweet Sorbet, Parakeet Party, Tahitian Tide, Orchid Oasis, Starry Sky

  • Images:  Altenew Sincere Greetings

  • Ink: Versafine Clair Nocturne

  • Dies:  Spellbinders Be Bold Alpha

  • Tools:  Perfect Layers; Fricken Square Corners; Mis-T Ruler
