Happy Monday crafty friends!  I am playing along with the challenge at Perfectly CAS today that wants to see a CAS card with embossing.  

I love the look of a hint of dry embossing on my creations and my first thought was to add a little sweater texture to a Christmas card.  I embossed an entire panel and used this strip for this card and the rest on the card I posted yesterday.

Card Info

*Paper:  Stampin’ Up! Plaid Tidings

*Image:  Mama Elephant Jingle Greetings

*Ink:  Versafine Clair Nocturne

*Embossing:  cable knit sweater

*Tools:  Perfect Layers

*Challenges:  Perfectly CAS #30
