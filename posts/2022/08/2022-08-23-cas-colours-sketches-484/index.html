*CAS Colours & Sketches #484*

#CC&S  #Design Team  #CAS  #Lawn Fawn  #Tim Holtz Distress Inks  #CAS Colours & Sketches  #Pink Fresh Studio 
August 23, 2022

It’s time for another sketch challenge at CAS Colours and Sketches.  You can rotate the sketch if you like, but we need to be able to see the essence of the sketch in your creation.  And those rectangles?  You can use any shape you want!

My card was going to be one layer… until I messed up the place of my splats so I trimmed down a front panel and adhered it to a new card.  I wasn’t sure about white on white layers for a while but now I see them as versatile to not only fix mistakes or hide ink that bleeds through (Copics), but they add a little bit of visual interest without being overwhelming on an otherwise clean and simple card.

Card Info

  • Images: Pink Fresh Studio Happy Birthday Sentiments

  • Ink: Tim Holtz Distress in Picked Raspberry, Mustard Seed, Twisted Citron, Salty Ocean, Shaded Lilac

  • Stencil:   Lawn Fawn Paint Splatter

  • Challenges:  CAS Colours & Sketches #484
