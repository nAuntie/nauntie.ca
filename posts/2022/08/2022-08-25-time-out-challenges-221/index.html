*Time Out Challenges #221*

#Nuvo Paste  #Catherine Pooler  #Design Team  #Mama Elephant  #Time Out Challenge  #CAS  #Christmas  #Taylored Expressions  #TO 
August 25, 2022

It’s time for a new challenge at Time Out Challenges with a wonderful prize from Birch Press Designs to be won!!  I hope you’ll play along, sharing your FESTIVE cards!!

I know that Festive can refer to a lot of different things, but to me, festive is Christmas… so I made a Christmas Card using a product I bought at the end of the season last year and hadn’t yet played with.  This is one of the cards I made using the Create in Quads stencils from Taylored Expressions.  I used some different colours than I normally would and I love how it turned out!  For the last layer, I used some glimmer paste to make it look like little bits of snow falling.  To created my coloured mat, I just swiped ink along a piece of white cardstock so that it would match the colour of ink I stencilled with.

Card Info

  • Images:  Mama Elephant Jingle Greetings

  • Ink:  Versafine Clair Nocturne;  Catherine Pooler Black Jack, Skylight

  • Dies:  MFT stitched circles

  • Tools:  Perfect Layers

  • Stencils:  Taylored Expressions Create in Quads Snowflake

  • Tools:  Taylored Expressions Blending Brushes

  • Etc:  Nuvo Glimmer Paste Moonstone
