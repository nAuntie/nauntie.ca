*CAS on Friday #238*

#CAS on Friday  #Catherine Pooler  #CAS  #Stampin' Up!  #PerfectLayers(We R Memory Keepers Layers Guides)  #Hero Arts 
August 28, 2022

I found a little time earlier this week to play along with the current TAG challenge at CAS on Friday.  I have had this idea in my head for a bit and finally put it together for this card.  I wanted to do it with just the tag on the card front to keep it more CAS but it just didn’t look right… I had this piece of embossed cardstock on my desk, the leftovers from a previous creation, so I tried it and loved it!

Card Info

  • Cardstock:  Stampin’ Up! Real Red, Basic Black

  • Images:  Catherine Pooler Nice List

  • Ink:  Memento Tuxedo Black; Versafine Clair Nocturne; Copics

  • Dies:  Catherine Pooler Nice List; Hero Arts Infinity Tags; Stampin' Up! Tasteful Labels

  • Etc: Stampin’ Up! embossing folder

  • Challenges:  CAS on Friday #238
