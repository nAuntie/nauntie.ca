*Paper Candles*

#CAS  #MFT  #Stampin' Up!  #My Favorite Things  #Versafine Clair 
August 15, 2022

I truly have a lot of patterned paper… and I honestly don’t know why since it often doesn’t fit in to a clean and simple style!  I think because it’s just so darn pretty!  I go through periods where I use it a lot and periods where I don’t use it much.  I think I need to go through a period where I don’t buy it.

Thanks so much to Nadine (@scrappinready on Instagram) for the inspiration!

Card Info

*Paper:  MFT Party Time

*Images:  Stampin’ Up! Biggest Wish

*Ink:  Versafine Clair Glamorous and Nocturne
