#Catherine Pooler  #LessIsMore  #Design Team  #LIM  #CAS  #MFT  #My Favorite Things 
October 8, 2022

It’s time for a new challenge at Less is More… we are playing with a one layer card which means we want ONLY ink on paper… no added die cuts, no layers.  The twist for this challenge is to draw your own frame.  Please share your creations with us HERE!

I shared my first creation for this challenge a few days ago… but then realized that I couldn’t use the die cut butterfly.  I was so disappointed because I was truly in love with that card!  I created a very similar frame for this card and did a little stamping to create a fun birthday card.
