*Build Your Stash Holiday Cardmakers*

#Design Team  #Stampin' Up!  #Build Your Stash  #Christmas  #Photoplay  #BYSHC  #Taylored Expressions  #Stickles 
October 14, 2022

I’m back today with another card following the October sketch at Build Your Stash Holiday Cardmakers.  I really love this sketch and could see going back to it a lot to use up some of my patterned papers.  Have you played along yet?

I made this one a little more simple with only one embossed layer and used a stamped image as the second panel.  I might have cheated a bit with that.  I really loved the plaid along the edge of the card but couldn’t find anything else I wanted to use as another layer that went with it.  So I skipped it!

Card Info

  • Cardstock:  Stampin’ Up! Cherry Cobbler

Paper:  Stampin’ Up! Plaid Tidings

  • Images:  Photoplay Gnome for the Holidays; Taylored Expressions Simple Strips Christmas

  • Ink:  Memento Tuxedo Black; Versafine Clair Nocturne; Copics

  • Dies:  Photoplay Gnome for the Holidays; Taylored Expressions Simple Strips, Simple Strips Backer

  • Embossing Folder:  Stampin’ Up!

  • Embellishments:  Stardust Stickles

  • Challenges:  Build Your Stash Holiday Cardmakers #27
