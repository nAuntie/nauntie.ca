*Addicted to CAS #230*

#Catherine Pooler  #AddictedtoCAS  #Design Team  #CAS  #PerfectLayers(We R Memory Keepers Layers Guides)  #Simon Says Stamp  #ATCAS 
October 1, 2022

There’s a new challenge at Addicted to CAS that starts today.  The code word is Autumn.  Play along with us by sharing you clean and simple cards HERE!

I had fun doing a little ink blending on a piece of cardstock and then stamping and cutting out these fun leaves.  I don’t use a lot of brown in my cards but an autumn theme and these colours called for some brown.  I like the red undertone of this cardstock.

Card Info

  • Cardstock:  Stampin’ Up! Cinnamon Cider

  • Images:  Right At Home Fall Leaves

  • Ink:  Versafine Clair Nocturne; Catherine Pooler Glitz, Flame, Mandarin Spice, Cranberry Fizz

  • Dies:  Right At Home Fall Leaves; Simon Says Stamp Big Thanks

  • Tools:  Perfect Layers

  • Challenges:  Addicted to CAS #230
