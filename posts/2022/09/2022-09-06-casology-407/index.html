It’s the first Tuesday of the month which means there is a new CASology challenge.  Our cue for this month is HAPPY!

The cue is easy for me as the majority of the cards I make are for birthdays… I pulled out some rainbow colours and an alphabet die set and created this for you!

Card Info

  • Cardstock:  Stampin’ Up! Basic Black, Poppy Parade, Daffodil Delight, Granny Apple Green, Pacific Point, Highland Heather

  • Dies:  Concord & 9th Tall Type Alpha; Hello Bluebird Happy Birthday Words; MFT Stitched Rectangles

  • Tools:  Perfect Layers
