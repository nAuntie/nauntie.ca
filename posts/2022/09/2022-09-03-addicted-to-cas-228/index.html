It’s time for a new clean and simple challenge at Addicted to CAS.  

This challenge was difficult for me…. because I don’t like birds!  I have a few in my stash but they don’t really get used.  But something different happened when the current Stampin’ Up! catalogue came out and I NEEDED these sweet bird stamps and the punch that goes with it!

Card Info

*Cardstock:  Stampin’ Up! Granny Apple Green, Bermuda Bay, Pumpkin Pie

*Images:  Stampin’ Up! Sweet Songbirds

*Ink:  Catherine Pooler Twilight, Over Coffee; Stampin’ Up! Parakeet Party; Versafine Clair Nocturne

*Punches:  Stampin’ Up! Sweet Songbirds

*Tools:  Perfect Layers
*Challenge:  Addicted to CAS #228
