It’s time for a word prompt challenge at Time Out Challenges.  We have a $25 gift card to Lawn Fawn up for grabs for one lucky player!!

This is such a beautiful quote by Maya Angelou…. and it’s something I try to do when I’m at work.  I work at the front desk of a chiropractic clinic and we often see people who are in a lot of pain.  I try had to be a bright spot in their day, maybe help them feel a little better for a few minutes while they have to interact with me.  Everyone needs a rainbow…

I started out my card with a panel of clouds and then added some sparkly grass and a slightly subdued rainbow…
