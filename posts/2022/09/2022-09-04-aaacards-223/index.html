*AAACards #223*

#Design Team  #CAS  #Hot Foil  #AAA Cards  #Spellbinders Hot Foil Glimmer Machine  #AAA  #Pink Fresh Studio  #Spellbinders 
September 4, 2022

It’s time for a no layer card challenge with AAACards… that’s right… just the card itself!  The optional twist is to make it a sympathy card.

Sympathy cards are not a card anyone wants to make… but they are necessary to have in our stash.  I personally like to make my sympathy cards subdued… I was curious to see how the silver foil would look on grey cardstock so that’s what I played with for my challenge card.  While my card looks to be just the card, I cheated just a bit and cut my grey cardstock to the same size as a folded white card and attached it to the front.  The only way you might know this isn’t a true no layer card is that the inside is white.  I like to do this as I prefer white on my card interior.
