*CAS Colours & Sketches #486*

#Catherine Pooler  #CC&S  #Design Team  #CAS  #Lawn Fawn  #CAS Colours & Sketches  #PerfectLayers(We R Memory Keepers Layers Guides) 
September 6, 2022

There’s a new host this month at CAS Colours & Sketches… Patricia has chosen these fun colours to play with this week!  I hope you’ll play along!

As you will often see me say, I struggled with these colours.  I don’t often put yellow on a card and certainly not with these other colours!  I wanted to play with ink for this challenge so I had to pull some similar colours from my stash and I added the Mango Melody in a layer.

Card Info

*Cardstock:  Stampin’ Up! Mango Melody

*Images:  Altenew Splash of Color

*Ink:  Versafine Clair Nocturne; Catherine Pooler Drive In and Pucker Up

*Stencils:  Lawn Fawn Paint Splatter

*Tools:  Taylored Expressions blending brushes; Perfect Layers

*Challenges:  CAS Colours & Sketches #486
