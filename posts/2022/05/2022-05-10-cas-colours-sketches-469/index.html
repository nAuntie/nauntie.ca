*CAS Colours & Sketches #469*

#CC&S  #Design Team  #CAS  #MFT  #Stampin' Up!  #CAS Colours & Sketches  #My Favorite Things  #Spellbinders  #Waffle Flower 
May 10, 2022

Now that I have a little more mobility (and I do mean a little) with my right hand, I decided to try making a card on yesterday afternoon.  Fine motor skills are still a bit of a struggle and I had to take a break part way through, but I managed to get a card done in time for the CAS Colours & Sketches challenge for this week.  

It’s a sketch challenge this week… I hope you’ll play along with us by sharing your clean and simple creations following the sketch below.  Just link that card up HERE!

I was thankful for my bin of extras as it helped me with the cut pieces.  All I had to do was cut the patterned paper, stamp the sentiment and put them all together 

Card Info

  • Cardstock:  Stampin’ Up! Real Red, Old Olive. Basic Black

  • Paper:  MFT Black & White Stripes

  • Images:  Spellbinders Sentiments for You

  • Ink:  Versafine Claire Nocturne

  • Dies:  Waffle Flower Additional Circles; Stampin’ Up Pierced Blooms

  • Challenges:  CAS Colours & Sketches #467

I don’t honestly know if I’ll be able to keep trying to make cards while I’m healing, but it was really nice to make a cad yesterday.  It’s been almost 4 weeks since I created… the longest I’ve gone without making a card in almost 5 years!  Using the hand/arm/wrist is important it getting motion and strength back but I want to be careful that I don’t overdo it!
