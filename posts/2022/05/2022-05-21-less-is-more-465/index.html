It’s time for a colour challenge over at the Less is More blog.  For this challenge we are playing with my favourite colour combination… RAINBOW!  There is some wonderful inspiration to be seen created by the rest of the design team.. click HERE to check them out!

I absolutely love creating with rainbows… they can be bright, muted, pastel, jewel toned… whatever you feel like!  I received my new Stampin’ Up! In Color cardstock right after I broke my wrist so I pulled those out (along with a few other colours) to create my rainbow for this challenge.

Card Info

  • Cardstock:  Stampin’ Up! Basic Black, Tangerine Tango, Daffodil Delight, Orchid Oasis, Parakeet Party, Sweet Sorbet, Tahitian Tide, Starry Sky, In Color Glimmer Pape

  • Images:  Pink Fresh Studio Small Butterflies, Happy Birthday Sentiments

  • Ink:  Versafine Clair Nocturne

  • Dies:  Pink Fresh Studio Small Butterflies; Lawn Fawn Everyday Sentiment Banners

  • Tools:  Perfect Layers; Fricken Square Corners

I really hope you’ll play along with us and share your clean and simple rainbow cards with us HERE.  I can’t wait to see all the beautiful colours!!
