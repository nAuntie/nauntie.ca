*Special Tulips*

#Fricken Square Corners  #slimline  #Crafty Meraki  #CAS  #Tim Holtz Distress Inks  #Pink Fresh Studio  #PerfectLayers(We R Memory Keepers Layers Guides) 
May 13, 2022

Happy Friday the 13th crafty friends!  If you’ve been here for a while, you know that my hubby and I celebrate Friday the 13th as we met (at a bar in the late 80s) on a Friday the 13th and got engaged on a Friday the 13th (our oldest son was born on the anniversary of the day Bruce proposed but it wasn’t a Friday).  We’ve been married 31 years (or it will be on June 1) so we figure it’s a pretty good day.

My card today is using some of the leftovers from the ink blended panels I created… this time I cut out some tulips and created a mini slimline!

Card Info

  • Cardstock:  Stampin’ Up! Lemon Lime Twist

  • Images:  Pink Fresh Studio Happy Birthday Sentiments

  • Ink:  Versafine Clair Nocturne;  Tim Holtz Distress in Villainous Potion, Twisted Citron, Peacock Feathers

  • Dies:  Crafty Meraki Flora Para-Dies

  • Tools:  Perfect Layers; Fricken Square Corners
